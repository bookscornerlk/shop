Why is Akuru Kiyaweema Ceremony?

අකුරු කියවීමට චාරිත්‍රයක් මොකටද ?

Akuru Kiyaweema (අකුරු කියවීම), which translates to "reading of the first letters," is an ancient Sri Lankan cultural ceremony that marks a young child's official introduction to formal literacy. Stemming from centuries-old traditions, this milestone ritual blends religious devotion, astrology, and family celebration to honor the beginning of a child's educational journey.

Planning the Akuru Kiyaweema can feel overwhelming. We’re here to help you focus on the joy of the moment by gathering all the traditional essentials for you, beautifully packaged and delivered island-wide.
